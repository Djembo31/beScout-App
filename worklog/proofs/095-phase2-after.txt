**RLS tighten on trades**:
- DROP `trades_select` (qual=true)
- CREATE `trades_select_own_or_platform_admin`:
  ```
  auth.uid() = buyer_id
  OR auth.uid() = seller_id
  OR EXISTS (profiles WHERE id = auth.uid() AND top_role = 'Admin')
  ```
