Query:
SELECT created_at, gameweek, step, status, details_preview
FROM cron_sync_log
WHERE step = 'no_past_fixtures' AND details::text ILIKE '%SL%'
ORDER BY created_at DESC LIMIT 3;

Results (2026-04-21 12:00-13:00 UTC):
- gameweek=31, step=no_past_fixtures, status=skipped, league=SL, reason="No fixtures past kickoff yet"
- gameweek=31 (wiederholt, 30-min-intervals)

→ Süper-Lig activeGw war bereits 31, obwohl GW 30 nicht komplett. Nur möglich

## DB-State (Slice 137 bereits dokumentiert)

SELECT gameweek, status, COUNT(*) FROM fixtures f
JOIN clubs c ON c.id=f.home_club_id AND c.league ILIKE '%süper%'
WHERE gameweek = 30 GROUP BY gameweek, status;

gameweek | status    | count
---------+-----------+------
      30 | finished  |     5
      30 | scheduled |     4   ← played_at 30-60h in Vergangenheit, aber "scheduled"

## Fix Analysis

Zeile 585 (vor Fix):
  allFixturesDone = fixtureCheck.allDone
  wobei fixtureCheck.allDone = (API.total > 0 && API.total === API.finished)

Problem: Wenn API-Football nur 5 statt 9 Fixtures für GW 30 Süper Lig zurückgab
(4 postponed/nicht eingereicht), dann:
  API.total=5, API.finished=5 → allDone=true
  DB.total=9, DB.finished=5 → 4 bleiben scheduled

Dennoch: Phase B führte `advance_gameweek` auf nextGw aus → 4 GW-30 Fixtures
sind seitdem unerreichbar.

## Fix (Zeile 766+)

Neue Guard nach Step 5b:
  const totalDbFixtures = mappings.fixtures.length;
  const postPhaseAFinishedCount = dbFinishedIds.size + newlyFinishedFixtures.length;
  const dbTruthAllDone = totalDbFixtures > 0 && postPhaseAFinishedCount >= totalDbFixtures;
  allFixturesDone = allFixturesDone && dbTruthAllDone;

Plus: Wenn API.allDone=true und DB.allDone=false → logStep 'phase_b_blocked_db_mismatch'
    für Monitoring (admin sieht cron_sync_log und kann sync-fixtures-future manuell triggern).
