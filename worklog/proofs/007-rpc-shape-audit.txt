PARSER NOTES (get_rpc_jsonb_keys)
=============================================================================
- Extracts keys from jsonb_build_object AND json_build_object calls.
- Records keys at call_depth = 1 (i.e., 1st/3rd/5th... string-literal args at
  the outermost build_object's paren depth).
- Sibling calls at depth 1 are also captured (multiple build_object statements
  in one RPC body → union of all their keys).
- Tracks comment ranges (-- line and /* block */) to avoid false positives.
- Tracks string-literal escaping (doubled '' → continued literal).
- Limitation: string-literal-returned JSON (`'{"a":1}'::JSONB`) is NOT parsed.
  These RPCs are excluded from INV-23 with documented reason (RPC_SHAPE_EXCLUDED).
