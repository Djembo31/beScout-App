## Silent-Fail-Audit Precision Limitation (documented)

The audit regex matches `.in('col', ...)` independently per line. After 087's fix, gameweek-sync:1254 has `.in('club_id', allLeagueClubIds)` on its own line with `.range(offset, offset + PAGE - 1)` on the line below. The audit counts this as HIGH even though the pattern is the correct fix.

Net: Slice 087 actually eliminated 2 real silent-fails (1 in gameweek-sync, 1 in footballData allSettled) but the audit count shows no improvement due to regex-per-line + Promise.allSettled not being one of the 6 tracked patterns. Better audit precision is a future /optimize iteration (separate slice).
