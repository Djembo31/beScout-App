# Gamification — 3-Dimensionales Elo-System

## 3 Dimensionen

| Dimension | Farbe | Trigger | Basis |
|-----------|-------|---------|-------|
| Trader | Sky Blue | Jeder Trade | Volumen, Profit-Percentile |
| Manager | Purple | Nach Gameweek Scoring | Ranking-Percentile |
| Analyst | Emerald | Community Content | Posts, Research, Bounties |

**Gesamt-Rang = MEDIAN(Trader, Manager, Analyst)** — nicht Durchschnitt

## 12 Tiers

```
1-3:   Bronze    (0-999, Subs I/II/III)
4-6:   Silber    (1000-2199)
7-9:   Gold      (2200-3999)
10:    Diamant   (4000-4999)
11:    Mythisch  (5000-6999)
12:    Legendaer (7000+)
```

## Features

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Achievements | 33 total (15 sichtbar, 18 hidden). Trading/Manager/Scout Kategorien |
| Missions | Taeglich/Woechentlich. assign → track → complete → claim |
| Streaks | Login-Streaks mit Shields. 3d=5, 7d=15, 14d=50, 30d=150 $SCOUT |
| Score Road | 11 Milestones mit BSD + Cosmetics (Frames, Titles, Avatars) |
